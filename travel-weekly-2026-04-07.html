A tale of two pressures: record inbound visitors (42.7M in 2025, Feb 2026 hit 3.46M monthly record) crashing against overtourism backlash and new regulatory clamps. Golden Week 2026 forecast shows volume growth (+1.9% to 24.47M trips) but first-ever per-capita spend decline (-2.1% to ¥46K). Generational split emerges: 60+ prefer history/nature, under-29 seek self-restoration, fan pilgrimages, digital detox, and sleep tourism.
