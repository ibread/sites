Prioritize K-content proximity listings and Chinese-language amenities for spillover demand.

Japan: Compliance urgency intensifies — April 1 Kyoto accommodation tax, July departure fee 3x, visa fees 5x increase signal regulatory tightening. 2026 national data-matching system targeting "ghost operators." Minpaku occupancy at 17% vs hotels 59-60%. Full compliance verification critical before peak season.
3. China: May Day preparation essential — Qingming holiday (Apr 4-6) showed 135M domestic trips (+6.8%); 175M outbound trips forecast for 2026 ($280B spend). Family travel dominates (37% of bookings). Partner with Korea/Thailand/Singapore hosts for Chinese guest readiness; optimize tier 2/3 domestic listings.
4. India: West Asia conflict headwind — ₹18,000 crore loss and 15-20% inbound decline require damage mitigation. However, domestic pivot continues (35% prioritizing domestic over international, up from 25%). Spiti Valley searches +340%; luxury domestic segment growing 3× faster than standard.
5. Regional redistribution opportunity — China's 165-175M outbound trips redirecting from Japan (-50%) to Korea, Vietnam, Thailand. Safety, visa ease, and direct flights now top decision factors. Airbnb should rebalance listing partnerships toward these beneficiary markets.
